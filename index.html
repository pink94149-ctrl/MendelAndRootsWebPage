About

Mendel and Roots Inc. is a Canadian start-up launched in 2026, focused on designing and building smart micro-farm technology for indoor, vertical, and hydroponic growing systems. Designed in Canada, the company develops certified hardware and software solutions that help automate plant care, monitor environmental conditions, and support reliable, consistent plant growth. Founded by a team driven by engineering discipline and a deep respect for sustainable food systems, Mendel and Roots Inc. bridges technology and cultivation to make advanced growing tools accessible to indoor growers, small farms, and people who grow fresh greens for personal use. Its products are designed with safety, reliability, and long-term use in mind.
